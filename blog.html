The kanchipuram pattu sarees

To ensure your precious Kanchipuram Silk Saree remains a treasured heirloom for generations, gentle care is crucial. Hand wash it in cold water using a mild silk detergent. Avoid harsh chemicals, bleach, and the rigors of machine washing. After washing, dry it in the shade on a flat surface to prevent stretching and color fading. Store your saree folded in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. To prevent creases and discoloration, interleave acid-free tissue paper between the folds

The Pochampalii pattu sarees

Pochampalli Ikat silk sarees are precious heirlooms demanding careful handling. Hand-wash them gently in cold water with a mild silk detergent. Avoid harsh chemicals and machine washing. Dry them in the shade to prevent color fading. Store them folded in a cool, dry place, interleaving with acid-free tissue paper. For stubborn stains, consider professional dry cleaning. By following these guidelines, you can ensure your saree remains beautiful for generations to come.

The Dharmavaram pattu sarees

Dharmavaram Pattu Sarees are renowned for their rich heritage and exquisite craftsmanship. Originating from Dharmavaram in Andhra Pradesh, India, these sarees are woven with pure silk and adorned with intricate zari and threadwork. Known for their bold geometric patterns, vibrant colors, and luxurious feel, Dharmavaram Pattu Sarees are a symbol of elegance and tradition. They are highly prized possessions, often passed down as heirlooms through generations, reflecting the enduring legacy of Indian textile artistry.
